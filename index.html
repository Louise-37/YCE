Traditional local supply chains face structural bottlenecks that cannot meet the high-density demands of AI projects.

NVIDIA H100/H200 clusters are pushing rack densities past 60kW. This structural shift creates three critical bottlenecks for traditional power distribution:

  • The Density Problem Legacy 208V systems require massive currents (200A+) for AI racks, creating unmanageable cable congestion that blocks airflow.
  • The "Stranded Power" Cost Without billing-grade metering (1%), operators over-provision power buffers, leaving up to 33% of expensive capacity unused.
  • The Heat Risk Thermal breakers de-rate in 60°C hot aisles, causing nuisance tripping that kills million-dollar training runs.
